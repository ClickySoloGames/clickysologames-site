About ClickySoloGames

ClickySoloGames is an independent HTML5 browser game developer focused on creating simple and engaging casual games. Our projects are designed to run directly in modern web browsers without installation.

We experiment with lightweight mechanics, minimalistic visuals, and fast-loading gameplay to ensure accessibility across desktop and mobile platforms.
